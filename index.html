About Us

Welcome to Radio Vivid Voice, the online radio station that brings you 24/7 commercial-free world music. No matter what kind of music you love, we have something for you. From Bengali, English, to Hindimusic, we play it all. You can also listen to Bengali talk shows and other fun programs that will keep you entertained and informed. Radio Vivid Voice is not just a radio station; it is a family of music lovers who share their passion and voice with the world. To join our family, you can visit our official website or use our Android app. You can also follow us on Facebook and Instagram to get the latest news and updates about our shows and events. Come on, join Radio Vivid Voice today, be a part of our beautiful family, and let the music speak to you! Let's ROCK together.
